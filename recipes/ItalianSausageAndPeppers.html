Italian Sausage and Peppers

Description

Sweet and Spicy Italian Sausage with red and green bell peppers and onions, all married together in a rich tomato sauce.

Enjoy on hoagie rolls as a sandwich, or just spoon it straight out of the bowl!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Italian Sausage (Sweet and Spicy)
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 2 green bell peppers
  • 2 large yellow onions
  • Many other things (I'm not typing all of this out)

Steps

  1. Brown the sausage on all sides (just browning--not cooking!)
  2. Take the sausages out and let them rest
  3. Throw the onions and peppers in and cook for a few minutes
  4. Add some garlic to it and cook for 30 seconds
  5. Add tomato paste and cook for 30 seconds
  6. Add the marsala wine and scrape the bottom of the pan
  7. Bring to a low simmer for a while
  8. Slice up the sausage into one-inch pieces and throw it in
  9. Let simmer until it's thickened and perf--about an hour
  10. Carve up some bread if you're so inclined and scoop it in, or just scoop into a bowl
  11. Enjoy!
