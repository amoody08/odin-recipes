Grilled Cheese

Description

I learned from my earlier mistake so this is a very simple one. I like cheese and bread so this is top marks.

I have been making these since I was a little kid, so anyone can do it!

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread--any kind!
  • Two slices of American cheese (don't get fancy)
  • Two little pats of butter

Steps

  1. Butter one side of each slice of bread
  2. Place one slice butter side down on a cold skillet
  3. Place both slices of cheese on the bread, top with other slice, butter side up
  4. Let cook, checking often, until bread is at desired brownness
  5. Flip it over, being careful not to spill the whole thing
  6. Cook second side, checking often, until bread is at desired brownness
  7. Hopefully the cheese melted, so take it off the heat
  8. Eat it. Maybe with some tomato soup.
